SmartSnack

Healthy snacks made smart

SmartSnack checks the product and the barcode of food and snacks.

Instant Ingredient Scanner

Scan any snack while shopping and instantly see a breakdown of its ingredients, additives, and hidden chemicals.

Allergy Alert System

Input your allergies once, and SmartSnack will automatically flag any risky ingredients when you scan a product.

Smart Alternatives

Get real-time suggestions for snacks.

Personalized Snack Suggestions

The app recommends snacks based on user-selected dietary preferences (e.g., vegan, gluten-free, low sugar).

Nutritional Breakdown & Comparison Tool

SmartSnack shows a visual breakdown of nutritional info for each snack and allows comparing two or more snacks.

Smart Pantry or Snack Tracker

Users can log snacks or build a “smart pantry” of their favorites or current inventory.
